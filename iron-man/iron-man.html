Phase One

Iron Man (2008)

Iron Man was released in 2008. This was the first movie released by
Marvel in the MCU but this is the second movie to watch in order after
Captain America: The First Avenger.

Tony Stark, the egotistical millionaire with a gift of advanced engineering
and science is captured during one of his sales to the military. This changed
his life forever as he fought his way out of captivity and became Iron Man.
