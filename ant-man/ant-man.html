Phase Two

Ant-Man (2015)

Before Phase Two Comes to an end we are introduced with
a new type of hero, a hero the size of an Ant.

A troubled man just after being released from prison has
been having a terrible life. Working somewhere he hates and
not being able see his child. In an attempt to get his life
back on track he gets back in the game, however encounters
a life changing suit.
